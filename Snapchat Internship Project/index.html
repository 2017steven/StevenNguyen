About Sable Valley Records

Sable Valley Records is a premier electronic dance music (EDM) record label that champions a diverse range of genres, including trap, dubstep, and more. Our label is dedicated to discovering and promoting the freshest and most innovative electronic artists in the industry, while providing unparalleled support and guidance to help them reach their full potential. Our artists are at the forefront of the latest EDM trends, regularly performing and promoting at the biggest events and festivals around the world. At Sable Valley Records, we pride ourselves on pushing boundaries and setting new standards in the EDM scene. Whether you're a fan of heart-thumping beats or intricate melodies, Sable Valley Records has something for everyone. Join us as we continue to redefine the world of electronic dance music, one release at a time.

About Our Founder

RL Grime, whose real name is Henry Steinway, is an American electronic music producer and DJ who is best known for his trap and bass music. He first rose to prominence in 2012 with his remix of "Mercy" by Kanye West, and has since become a staple in the electronic music scene. In 2018, RL Grime launched Sable Valley Records, a record label that aims to showcase forward-thinking electronic music and provide a platform for emerging artists. The label's name is a nod to RL Grime's love of nature and the great outdoors. Since its inception, Sable Valley has become a major player in the electronic music world, releasing music from both established artists and up-and-comers. RL Grime's own releases, including his album "Nova," have also been released on the label. Overall, RL Grime's vision for Sable Valley is to create a community of artists and fans who share a passion for innovative electronic music. With his talent and dedication, he has undoubtedly cemented himself as one of the most influential figures in the genre today.
